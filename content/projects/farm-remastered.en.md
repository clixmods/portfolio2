# Farm - Remastering

## Project Description

Farm is a remaster of a zombie map of the same name from Call Of Duty: Black Ops 2 for Call of Duty: Black Ops 3. This modding project focuses on the visual and technical improvement of an existing environment, using the modern capabilities of the engine to create a renewed experience.

## Level Building and Construction

### Modern Reconstruction
- **Analysis** of the original map
- **Complete reconstruction** with Black Ops 3 tools

### Advanced Construction Techniques
- **Precise modeling** of environments
- **Enriched architectural details**
- **Optimized geometry** for rendering
- **Precise collision meshes** for gameplay

## Research and Development

### Advanced Modding Techniques
- **Exploration** of BO3 engine possibilities
- **Experimentation** with lighting tools
- **Performance optimization** without sacrificing quality

## Results

Remastered FARM demonstrated my ability to modernize and improve existing environments while respecting their original essence. This project strengthened my understanding of advanced lighting techniques and 3D environment construction.

The map was well received by the community for its visual quality and attention to detail, establishing an initial reputation.

## Acknowledgments

Special thanks to Treyarch for creating the original, and to all the modders and artists whose tools and assets were essential in bringing this remaster to life.
