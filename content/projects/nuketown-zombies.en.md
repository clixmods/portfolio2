# Nuketown Zombies

>"Mass-energy equivalence, secret tests, crash-landing perks. Survive in the iconic Nuketown, where the past and the future come together."

Complete remaster of the iconic Nuketown Zombies map from Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 for Call of Duty: Black Ops 3. This ambitious project represents much more than a simple port: it's a faithful recreation that brings revolutionary new features to the Zombies mode, including support for up to 8 players and the integration of intelligent bots.

## Creative Vision

After the success of FARM, I chose to tackle Nuketown Zombies because it was a map I particularly enjoyed on Black Ops 2, despite the unfortunate absence of a quest or secrets even though its place in Zombies mode history offered narrative potential.

The initial goal was to create a remake rather than a simple remaster, preserving the fundamental gameplay while greatly extending the map's potential with additional areas and a quest consistent with the game's universe.

## Advanced Technical Development

### First In-Depth Programming Experience
This project marked my true initiation into the world of programming. Nuketown featured complex gameplay elements that required a technical approach.

### Script Conversion and Adaptation
- **Export** of original Black Ops 2 scripts via community tools
- **In-depth analysis** of all files to understand the logic
- **Manual conversion** of code for Black Ops 3 compatibility
- **Adaptation** to differences between game engines
- **Correction** and optimization

## Innovation: Zombies Experience

### Revolution of Zombies Mode
This project introduced revolutionary features that would later become the foundation of the **Zombies Experience** project:

#### 8-Player Support
- **World's first** in custom zombies at the time
- **Unlocking** the initial 4-player limitation
- **Performance optimization** for extended matches
- **Gameplay balancing** for large teams

#### Intelligent Bot System
- **Programming** of bot AI
- **Behaviors** adapted to Zombies mode
- **Solo play capability** with AI

#### Advanced Customization
- **Game feel choice**: Black Ops 2 or Black Ops 3 (reproduction of mechanics from the previous game)
- **HUD customization** according to preferences between different Black Ops games (1, 2, and 3)
- **Customizable weapon kits** for each playstyle (each player could choose the weapons they wanted to use during their game, an unprecedented feature)
- **Fully configurable match settings** such as difficulty

## Creation Process

### Analysis and Design Phase
- **In-depth study** of the original architecture
- **Use** of developers' standard scale documents for doors, stairs, etc.
- **Import of assets** from the original map for reference

### Blockout and Technical Foundation
- **Creation** of an accurate blockout faithful to the original
- **Respect** for proportions and gameplay flow
- **Adaptation** to Black Ops 3 technical standards
- **Optimization** from the design phase

## Level Design and Construction

### Faithful Recreation of Nuketown
- **Visual reproduction** of buildings
- **Adaptation** to the game's standard scale
- **Preservation** of original gameplay
- **Improvement** of geometry for performance

### Technical Construction
- **Precise modeling** of buildings and terrain
- **Collision optimization** for fluid gameplay

## Content and Features

### Graphical Improvements
- **High-quality retexturing**: New textures for PBR materials adapted to the Black Ops 3 engine
- **Modernized models**: 3D assets updated to graphics standards
- **Enhanced visual effects**: Particles and lighting
- **Aesthetic consistency**: Harmonization with the Black Ops 3 universe

### Advanced Programming
- **Gameplay scripts** entirely rewritten and optimized
- **Integration** of Nuketown-specific mechanics
- **Compatibility** with external mod systems

### Visual and Audio Effects
- **Custom FX system** for post-apocalyptic atmosphere
- **Immersive sound design**
- **Dynamic lighting** to enhance atmosphere
- **Post-processing** for final aesthetics

## Impact and Legacy

### Community Success
With over 600,000 downloads and a place in the top 10 of the Black Ops 3 Workshop, Nuketown Zombies marked the community with its ambition and execution quality. Positive feedback from players and content creators confirmed the relevance of technical and artistic choices.

### Technical Revolution
This project introduced for the first time 8-player support and an intelligent bot system in Zombies modding, innovations that inspired many creators and established new standards for the community.

### Foundational Learning
Nuketown marked a turning point in my journey as a developer:
- **Introduction to programming**: First in-depth experience with complex scripts
- **Technical mastery**: Understanding of game engines and development tools
- **Methodology**: Structured approach to video game development
- **Professional vision**: Transformation of a passion into a career
