## About the project

Vigor Sport is a WordPress showcase website built for Romain Garro, a practitioner specializing in sports massage and muscle recovery, offering home services around Saint-Andre-de-Sangonis, France. The site was designed to clearly position the business, build trust in the professional framework, and convert visitors into contacts or booking requests.

## Structure and content

The homepage is built around a clear and direct hero section with a dual call-to-action, followed by a readable structure: key benefits, service overview, session walkthrough, trust elements, coverage areas, FAQ and a final CTA. The goal is to make the offering immediately understandable without overwhelming the user with too much information.

The visual approach is premium, clean and sports-oriented, with messaging centered on recovery, muscle release, sport-specific targeting and trust. The site also highlights essential points to remove friction: impeccable hygiene, professional equipment, adaptation to each athlete, home visits, and an explicit reminder of the professional nature of the services.

## Services and offerings

Content is structured into several service blocks: classic wellness, pre-workout, post-workout, combined pre/post, as well as a dedicated team package with a quote-based model. Pricing, duration and benefits are presented simply to ease decision-making and minimize friction at the booking stage.

## Booking flow

The site features a guided booking flow with a session request form, service selection, preferred time slots, contact information and a confirmation message. A dedicated section for coverage areas details the travel logistics and potential fees outside the free zone, reinforcing service transparency.
