# Zombies Experience

Zombies Experience is a mod for Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 that transforms the gaming experience. This ambitious project considerably extends the capabilities of the original game by adding new features.

## Main Features

### 8-Player Multiplayer Extension
- **Engine modification** to support 8 simultaneous players
- **Performance optimization** to maintain fluidity
- **Compatibility** with existing maps and those created by players

### Multiple Game Modes
- **New game modes** created specifically for 8 players
- **Variants** of classic modes adapted
- **Balancing** of mechanics for larger groups

## Advanced Technical Integrations

### Discord Rich Presence
- **Integration via wrapper**
- **Real-time display** of game status
- **Detailed information**: map, mode, players
- **Direct invitations** to matches from Discord

### Weapon Kits
- **Extended arsenal** with weapons from different Black Ops games (1, 2, 3, and 4)
- **Per-player customization** of weapon kits
- **Balancing** between different generations
- **Saved preferences** for each player

## Technical Development

### Advanced C++ Programming
- **Code injection** via DLL

## Research and Development

### Reverse Engineering
- **In-depth analysis** of the Black Ops 3 engine
- **Understanding** of internal systems
- **Identification** of extension points

### Technical Innovation
- **Creative solutions** to engine limitations
- **Workarounds** for multiplayer restrictions
- **Stability** in unforeseen conditions

## Content Management

### Dynamic Map Playlist System
- **Real-time display** of mod-compatible maps directly in-game

## Community Impact

### Adoption and Usage
- **Active community** of regular players
- **Dedicated servers** using the mod with over 2,000 players at its peak
- **Continuous feedback** for improvement

## Technical Challenges Overcome

### Engine Limitations
- **Workarounds** for hardcoded 4-player limits
- **Stabilization** of extended networking
- **Maintenance** of original performance
- **Compatibility** with original and community-created custom content

## Results and Recognition

Zombies Experience transformed the Black Ops 3 experience for thousands of players, demonstrating technical mastery in modding and development, as well as an ability to create innovative solutions to existing technical limitations.

### Community Influence
This project served as inspiration and technical foundation for other major mods in the Black Ops 3 community, some of which achieved even greater success. The engine modification and multiplayer extension techniques developed for Zombies Experience established new standards for advanced modding in the community.
