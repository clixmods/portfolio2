# Zombies Chronicles 2 - A Gift from Fans to Fans

After years of exchanging, creating, and sharing with the modding community on **Call of Duty: Black Ops 3**, we wanted to offer something special to players: **Zombies Chronicles 2**, a free DLC created by fans... for fans.

In 2017, Treyarch released the official *Zombies Chronicles* DLC: eight remastered maps, a true visual masterpiece and a concentrated dose of nostalgia. But some essentials were missing... **TranZit, Die Rise, Buried, and Nuketown** didn't get their comeback.

{{< figure src="/images/posts/zombies-chronicles.webp" alt="Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 - Zombies Chronicles - official artwork" caption="Zombies Chronicles - promotional artwork from Call of Duty: Black Ops 3" width="480" >}}

With passion and perseverance, alongside talented creators from around the world, we set out to **remaster these legendary maps**. Entirely voluntary work, driven by love for this game mode that punctuated so many nights of our youth.

## Project Goals

- Preserve the original spirit of the maps
- Modernize visuals, lighting, and assets
- Fix certain flows or ergonomic issues
- Offer a cohesive pack accessible for free

## A Community Adventure

This project was built on:
- Very active international Discord servers
- Constant exchanges between level designers, scripters, and artists
- Community playtesting at every iteration
- Modding tools pushed to their maximum potential

> It's this community that taught me, inspired me, and led me to where I am today.
